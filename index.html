The Little learner Difference

Nutritious Menu

Child nutrition is paramount. We serve quality kosher meals, ensuring freshness and balance under the State Food Program (CACFP). Every meal supports your child's well-being

Voucher programs

Recognizing the cost of day care, we accept ACS/HRA Vouchers. Every family can access our top-notch care, upholding our high standards irrespective of finances.

Embracing early learning

In collaboration with NYC DOE, we provide free Universal Pre-K. Blending the DOE curriculum with our activities, we promote quality education and enrich our community's future.

Parent Partnership & updates

We partner with parents for tailored care at our daycare. Through Brightwheel app parents recieve updates and communicate with our team. Face-to-face chats are also valued.

STEM adventures

Beyond academics, our daycare nurtures vital skills. In our center, children explore STEM, embracing science, technology, engineering, and fun math in a diverse setting.

Family of compassionate educators

Our teachers are caregivers and a family. Offering personalized care, our hiring ensures a team committed to top-tier childcare. Choose little learners for premier childcare!
